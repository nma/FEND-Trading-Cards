Platypus

Male platypi have venom glands which deliver its payload through a spur in their hind legs.

  • Scientific Name: Ornithorhynchus anatinus
  • Average Length: 50 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 17 years (In captivity) 7 years (In wild)
  • Habitat: River Banks

This animal is only found in the eastern coast of Australia. It is one of five species of mamals that lay eggs instead of giving birth. They are carnivore that feeds on worms, insects and crustceans.
